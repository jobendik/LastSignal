# LAST SIGNAL — Long Description

**The last relay station between humanity and the void is going dark. You are its only defender.**

Last Signal is a tactical sci-fi roguelite tower defense set across seven escalating sectors of deep space. Every run is different: random modifiers reshape each sector, signal upgrades define your build, and the enemy never attacks the same way twice.

## Features

- **7 Campaign Sectors** — from a shattered orbital relay to the Blackout Array itself, each with hand-authored waves, boss threats, and primary/secondary objectives.
- **Endless Mode** — after clearing any sector, push an infinite scaling gauntlet and compete on the global leaderboard for highest wave reached.
- **Void Sector** — a brutal 15-wave remix featuring all three campaign bosses in sequence. The hardest challenge in the game.
- **10 Tower Types** — Pulse, Stasis, Mortar, Tesla, Flamer, Railgun, Drone Hub, Harvester, Reflector, Shield. Every tower has two specialization paths.
- **Signal Upgrade System** — at the end of each wave, choose from three run-defining upgrades. No two runs play the same.
- **Run Modifiers & Curses** — random sector modifiers add hazards: jammer fields, reduced credits, enemy speed surges. Difficulty scales with your choices.
- **Research Array** — persistent meta-progression lets you unlock Endless Mode, improve resource yields, and customize your starting loadout.
- **Command Squads** — deploy recon, engineer, and strike teams to capture strategic relays and repair damaged towers.
- **Full Accessibility** — high-contrast palette options, UI scale, reduced motion mode.
- **CrazyGames Leaderboards** — endless wave leaderboard and Sector 7 speed-run ranking.

## How to Play

Place towers on grid nodes to intercept enemy waves before they reach the Core. Earn credits by killing enemies. Between waves, upgrade towers and choose signal upgrades. Survive all waves to clear the sector.

Losing the Core ends the run — but your research progress carries over.

## Controls

Mouse to build, click to select, keyboard shortcuts for every action. Full mobile touch support on tablets and phones.
